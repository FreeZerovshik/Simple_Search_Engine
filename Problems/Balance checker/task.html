Balance checker

Your task is to create an algorithm that prevents e-wallet accounts of your customers to go below zero.

Input contains:

  1. On the first line, there is a single integer number which is the initial amount of money on the account.
  2. The second line contains a sequence of purchases (integer numbers in a row).

Output contains:

  1. If there is no money for the purchase: "Error, insufficient funds for the purchase. You have N, but you need M." where N is the amount of money on the account at the moment, M is the amount of money you wanted to spend.
  2. If there is enough money for all purchases, print this: "Thank you for choosing us to manage your account! You have N money.", where N is the amount of money after all purchases.

Sample Input:
10000
5000 3000

Sample Output:
Thank you for choosing us to manage your account! You have 2000 money.


Sample Input:
1000
500 500

Sample Output:
Thank you for choosing us to manage your account! You have 0 money.


Sample Input:
5000
500 2000 3000

Sample Output:
Error, insufficient funds for the purchase. You have 2500, but you need 3000.
